About Ideal Public School

Nurturing Minds, Building Character, Inspiring Excellence.

ESTD. 2007

Our Journey

Since our establishment in 2007, Ideal Public School has stood as a cornerstone of academic brilliance. We believe that true education is a harmonious blend of modern innovation and timeless values.

Under the divine blessings of Saraswati Mata, we provide an environment where every student is encouraged to grow intellectually, physically, and spiritually.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, high-quality learning environment that empowers students to reach their full potential through a balanced curriculum of technology and tradition.

Our Vision

To be a premier institution recognized for producing ethical, creative, and courageous leaders who contribute positively to the global community.

The I.D.E.A.L. Way

Our culture is defined by five foundational pillars

I

Integrity

Doing the right thing, even when no one is watching.

D

Discipline

Cultivating the self-control and focus required for success.

E

Empathy

Developing a compassionate heart and respect for all perspectives.

A

Achievement

Striving for excellence in academics, sports, and the arts.

L

Leadership

Taking initiative to inspire and create positive change.

Admission Essentials

Ensure a smooth enrollment process by preparing these documents in advance.

Birth Certificate

Original government-issued proof of age for verification.

Transfer Certificate

Required (TC) for students migrating from another school.

Academic Records

Previous report cards or transcripts (last 2 years).

Identity Proof

Aadhaar cards of the student and both parents.

Address Proof

Recent utility bill, rent agreement, or passport copy.

Photographs

6 recent passport-sized photos in school uniform/formals.

Note: Please bring original documents along with 2 sets of self-attested photocopies.
